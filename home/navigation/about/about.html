Who We Are

NepYatra is a leading vehicle rental platform based in Nepal. Our mission is to make renting vehicles simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone.

We offer a wide range of vehicles, from budget-friendly options to luxurious rides, ensuring our customers find the perfect vehicle for their journey.

Our Mission

At NepYatra, we aim to:

  • Provide a seamless vehicle rental experience.
  • Offer the best pricing and widest selection of vehicles.
  • Support sustainable tourism and local businesses in Nepal.
